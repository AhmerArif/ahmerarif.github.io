Faculty Profile

Ahmer Arif

Assistant professor in the School of Information at the University of Texas at Austin. I study how communication technologies shape civic discourse, how disinformation spreads, and how we can design for reflection and care in sociotechnical systems.

View publications Connect

  • Position: Assistant Professor, UT Austin

  • Focus: Disinformation, reflection, platform governance
  • Collaborations: Facebook, Yahoo!, The World Bank, UN

Research agenda

I examine how people navigate, interpret, and respond to information across online spaces, with a focus on disinformation and collective sensemaking.

Disinformation & civic discourse

Understanding how narratives take hold, how communities respond, and what interventions can support healthier public conversations.

Reflective experiences

Designing moments of pause that help people notice their participation in information ecosystems and their broader impacts.

Platform accountability

Exploring socio-technical practices, policy, and interface design that can make moderation and governance more transparent.
